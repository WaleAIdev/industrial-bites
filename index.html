A Feast of Flavours in an Iconic Setting

Industrial Bites transforms the historic Areal Böhler — once a steel‑forging powerhouse, now one of Europe’s most spectacular industrial event campuses — into a vibrant culinary playground. Wander through soaring factory halls and open‑air courtyards as you sample bold street‑food, craft beverages, and sweet treats from over 60 vendors representing every corner of the globe.

Beyond bites, enjoy live music, chef demos, and pop‑up art celebrating Düsseldorf’s creative spirit. Families are welcome (kids under 12 eat free!), and the entire site is barrier‑free and dog‑friendly. All of this set against the raw steel beams and brick facades that give Areal Böhler its unmistakable charm.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to Areal Böhler?

Take tram line U76 to D‑Lörick, right at the site entrance, or set your GPS to Hansaallee 321, 40549 Düsseldorf. 1,500 on‑site parking spots and EV chargers are available.

Is the event cash‑free?

Yes. All vendors accept contactless card and mobile payments.

Are pets allowed?

Friendly dogs on leash are welcome in outdoor areas.

Will there be vegetarian/vegan options?

Absolutely — at least 40% of stalls offer vegan dishes.
